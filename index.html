About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Carisa Saenz-Videtto. I am 25 years old with life that is nothing short to say wonderful. I was born in Tucson, AZ raised in Phoenix, AZ.

Currently, my activities include helping those in need, training for a marathon, listening to any kind of music as long as it has the right beat, and spending time with my friends and family.

In 2018 I enrolled for Web Development at the University of Arizona. To date coding and educating myself on different aspects of front-end design has shown a light in my passions for technology. As your browse through my portfolio you'll see the learning growth in the program. My portfolio is always updating so stay tuned.
